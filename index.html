I am a PhD student at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems (Tübingen, Germany), and I am happy to be co-supervised by Moritz Hardt and Bob Williamson. My research investigates sociotechnical systems, focusing on the interface between Large Language Models (LLMs) and society. Specifically, I study how LLMs function within social media platforms and the broader research ecosystem, aiming to uncover both how these models reflect reality and how they actively shape it.
